EVENTS

Halloween Costume Contest

Every year the Grand Illusion hosts a Halloween costume contest! Come on over with your best Halloween costume that you've got! The contest is judged by a variety of different aspects of your costume including spookiness, kookiness, execution, style, etc. Whatever catches the eyes of our employees shall determine who the true winner is! Come on by and dress for success every Halloween, here at the G.I.!

Trucker's Thanksgiving

Come by to the Grand Illusion for Trucker's Thanksgiving on Thursday, November 25 from 7pm to 1:45am! What's better than a day with the family? A night with your second family! Don your best trucker hat and join us for a night of old school country music. Rail and second shelf cocktails ONLY $2.00 until MIDNIGHT!

Doodle Sunday's

Did you know? Every Sunday at The Grand Illusion is "Doodle Sunday." Think you have what it takes to win? Come flex your art muscles or just go straight back to Kindergarten every Sunday night here at the Grand Illusion! At the end of the night, we go through all of the drawings and knight the best looking one as winner!

Live Music Night

Every once in a while the G.I. will host local musicians and bands to come play some live music for all of our bar-goers! If announced, come on over to the G.I. to bang and rock out to some live music while you enjoy a delusion or two at the bar!
