You probably don't have to say this twice, but we got the stiffest drinks in Eau Claire for great prices! You don't wanna miss the main reason why we're here! We are also proud to be the home of the infamous Delusion!

Super Social

Here at the Grand Illusion, we believe that sociability is the most important aspect of going out to bars! Due to our heavy beliefs, we have a zero television policy to make for the most socially inclined environment on Water Street!

Here at the G.I., we serve a specially alchemized brew titled the Delusion. The Delusion is a secret family recipe that is guaranteed to get you feeling deluded; whatever that means to you. Delusions are $5.00, and every time you purchase one, you receive a special blue delusion ticket. These tickets can be traded in for some G.I. drip! Collect 10 delusion tickets for an exclusive Delusion Tee!

Popcorn!

Another 'lil feature we love to flaunt around a bit here and there is our free popcorn! Treat yourself with some free popcorn whilst sipping on your delusion for the ultimate combo!
